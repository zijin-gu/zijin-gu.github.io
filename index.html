Zijin Gu

I am Zijin Gu, a PhD student in ECE at Cornell University working with Prof. Amy Kuceyeski and Prof. Mert Sabuncu. I use tools from machine learning, statistics and brain imaging to solve neuroscience problems.

I interned at Pinterest in summer 2022 as a Machine Learning Research Intern where I worked on revenue prediction using ML models.

Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree at Zhejiang University, where I worked with Prof. Yong Ding on 3D image quality assessment. I also minored in Advanced Honors Class of Engineering Education (ACEE) at Chu Kochen Honors College. I visited UC Davis as a summer research intern advised by Prof. Soheil Ghiasi at Laboratory for Embedded and Programmable Systems.
